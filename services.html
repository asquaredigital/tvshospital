Pediatrics

Our pediatrics department offers comprehensive care for infants, children, and adolescents. We focus on preventive health care, the diagnosis and treatment of acute and chronic diseases, and the monitoring of physical and mental development.

Our services include:

  • Regular health check-ups
  • Vaccinations
  • Growth and development assessments
  • Nutrition and diet advice
  • Management of common illnesses
  • Specialist referrals when necessary

Pediatric Surgery

Our pediatric surgery department provides specialized surgical care for children, addressing both congenital and acquired conditions. Our team of expert surgeons is dedicated to delivering the highest standard of care in a child-friendly environment.

Our services include:

  • Corrective surgery for congenital anomalies
  • Minimally invasive surgery
  • Emergency surgical care
  • Postoperative care and rehabilitation
  • Parental guidance and support

Gynaecology and Obstetrics

Our gynaecology and obstetrics department provides comprehensive care for women's reproductive health. From routine check-ups to complex surgeries, our team is dedicated to ensuring the well-being of women at every stage of life.

Our services include:

  • Annual gynecological exams
  • Pregnancy care and delivery
  • Fertility treatments
  • Menopause management
  • Gynecological surgeries
  • Family planning and contraceptive advice

Obstetrics

Our obstetrics department is dedicated to providing comprehensive prenatal, childbirth, and postpartum care. We aim to support mothers through every stage of pregnancy, ensuring both mother and baby receive the best possible care.

Our services include:

  • Prenatal check-ups and monitoring
  • Labor and delivery support
  • High-risk pregnancy management
  • Postpartum care and support
  • Lactation consulting
  • Parental education classes

Orthopedic

Our orthopedic department offers advanced treatments for musculoskeletal issues. We provide care for injuries and disorders affecting the bones, joints, ligaments, tendons, and muscles.

Our services include:

  • Joint replacement surgery
  • Arthroscopic surgery
  • Fracture care
  • Sports medicine
  • Spine surgery
  • Rehabilitation and physical therapy

ENT Surgery

Our ENT (Ear, Nose, and Throat) surgery department provides expert care for a wide range of conditions. Our specialists use the latest techniques to treat both common and complex ENT disorders.

Our services include:

  • Sinus surgery
  • Ear surgery
  • Throat and voice surgery
  • Treatment for sleep apnea
  • Allergy testing and treatment
  • Pediatric ENT care

Neurology and Neuro Surgery

Our neurology and neurosurgery department offers comprehensive care for neurological disorders. We provide both surgical and non-surgical treatments for conditions affecting the brain, spine, and nervous system.

Our services include:

  • Stroke management
  • Epilepsy treatment
  • Brain and spine surgery
  • Management of neurological disorders
  • Neurorehabilitation
  • Chronic pain management

General Medicine

Our general medicine department provides a broad range of medical services for adults. We focus on the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of various health conditions.

Our services include:

  • Routine health check-ups
  • Management of chronic diseases
  • Acute illness treatment
  • Preventive care and screenings
  • Nutritional counseling
  • Health education and promotion

Our services include:

  • Laparoscopic surgery
  • Gastrointestinal surgery
  • Hernia repair
  • Appendectomy
  • Gallbladder surgery
  • Postoperative care and follow-up
